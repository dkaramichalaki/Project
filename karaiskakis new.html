War Consequences

An escalation of the conflict in Ukraine would have "catastrophic" global economic consequences. In addition to the war itself, the sanctions imposed on Russia would also have a "substantial impact on the global economy and financial markets, with knock-on effects for other countries.", notes the Fund. "The economic consequences are already very serious," he added. The rise in the price of energy and raw materials in general, with oil now approaching $120 a barrel, is intensifying the inflationary pressures that the whole world was facing coming out of the pandemic. "Rising prices will have consequences for the whole world, especially for the poorest households, for whom expenditure on energy and food constitutes the largest proportion" of their budget. As for Ukraine itself, it is "already clear" that the country is facing significantly increased costs associated with rebuilding the buildings and infrastructure that were destroyed or damaged.
